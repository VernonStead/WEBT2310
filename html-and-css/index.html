Braided Updo

This style involves taking the braided hair typically a french braid or a dutch braid, than you coil the braid into a bun at the nape of the neck (or on the top of the head).

Dutch Braid

The Dutch braid is one of the more common braids, its a three strand braid but instead of the hair being crossed over eachother they are crossed under eachother.

This is an example of a single dutch braid, its the exact same as the dutch braid were you go under instead of over.
